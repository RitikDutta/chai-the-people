# Product Requirements Document: Chai The People ## 1. Project Overview **Chai The People** is a civic-tech and local economic empowerment platform that uses India’s daily ritual of chai drinking as a gateway for civic participation. By scanning a QR code at participating tea stalls, users answer short surveys in exchange for a free cup of chai. ### 1.1 Core Mission 1. **Gather Citizen Insights:** Collect localized, anonymized public opinion data. 2. **Support Local Vendors:** Increase footfall and provide economic incentives to small tea stalls. 3. **Empower Voices:** Make civic participation accessible and rewarding for everyday people. 4. **Inform Policy:** Generate aggregated survey intelligence for policymakers and researchers. --- ## 2. Brand Identity & Visual Strategy * **Personality:** Warm, welcoming, rooted, optimistic, trustworthy, community-focused. * **Color Palette:** * Milk Tea (#F7E7CE) * Chai Brown (#8B5E3C) * Masala Terracotta (#C96A3D) * Elaichi Green (#6E8B3D) * Saffron Accent (#D9A441) * Deep Charcoal (#2F241F) * **Typography:** Elegant modern headlines with clean, accessible body text. * **Design Language:** Rounded cards, soft textures, subtle Indian context, modern product sensibility. --- ## 3. User Flows & Stakeholders ### 3.1 Citizens (The App Users) * **Flow:** Visit Stall → Scan QR → Register/Login → Take Survey → Claim Free Chai. * **Value Prop:** Simple way to be heard, rewarded with a daily ritual. ### 3.2 Shop Owners (The Partners) * **Flow:** Register Stall → Manage IDs → Track Participation → View Footfall Stats. * **Value Prop:** Increased business, digital visibility, community status. ### 3.3 Administrators (The Managers) * **Flow:** Create Questions → Manage Users/Roles → Define Stall Targeting → View KPIs. * **Value Prop:** High-quality, real-world data collection and management. --- ## 4. Technical Architecture * **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS (ES6) / Next.js (Modern implementation). * **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions). * **Analytics:** Chart.js for data visualization. * **Security:** Role-based access control (RBAC), Firestore security rules, anonymized data aggregation. --- ## 5. Homepage Structure (Planned) 1. **Hero:** "Turning a cup of chai into a voice for the people." 2. **The Problem:** Bridging the gap between civic opinion and policy. 3. **The Solution:** The QR-to-Chai engine. 4. **How It Works:** 6-step visual guide. 5. **Impact:** Cards for Citizen Voice, Vendor Support, Policy Support. 6. **Ecosystem:** Stakeholder profiles. 7. **Product Features:** QR Access, Role Dashboards, Dynamic Surveys. 8. **Technical Deep Dive:** Architecture and Data Model. 9. **Security & Privacy:** Trust-building narrative. 10. **Future Roadmap:** Gamification, Advanced Insights. 11. **Social Impact:** The broader vision of grounded civic interaction. 12. **Final CTA:** Start the conversation.
